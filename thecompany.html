Our Story

Dine Rite Seating has been creating custom, beautiful furniture for restaurants and commercial spaces for over 40. Our focus on crafting custom, luxurious installations elevate any space without taking the center stage. Whether you're a New York City five-star restaurant or a beloved local diner, Dine Rite Seating is eager to partner with you to create the perfect space.

The Team

The Shop

Dine Rite Seating manufactures all of its furniture custom made using the old-fashioned way: handcrafted with high quality materials and the finest workmanship.

We understand that the design process is an evolution and adventure, and we work with you to find a way to realize your vision. Together with architects, restaurant owners, interior designers, contractors and millworkers, Dine Rite Seating cultivates a partnership that doesn't end when we deliver your order.

Our Mission

At DRS, our goal is to better serve, inspire and educate people in regards to custom commercial furniture and upholstery. Our blog gives us an opportunity to interact directly with you. Here, we share our expertise, our newest educational content, our most recent projects, and highlight some of favorite architects and designers.
