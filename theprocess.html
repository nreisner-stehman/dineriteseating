It all starts with an idea...

1

The Consultation

We start off by sitting down and discussing your vision and goals of the project. We want to know more about your team, understand your expectations, budget expectations, and timeline.

2

Sketching and Drawing

We create designs and sketches for the project. We then share them with you for feedback. Since this is a partnership, we keep in touch with you and your team throughout the process.

3

Engineering Drawings

We create the designs in our engineering software, AutoCAD, including layout plans and furniture designs. At this stage, the team decides on materials and finishes.

4

Fabrication

This is the exciting part. We build your furniture in house with our wide variety of equipment and talented team.

5

Installation

The DRS team brings the furniture to your business. We set up and build your furniture ot the drawings and specifications that were designed.

6

Follow Up

We stay in touch with you for any issues or problems with your installation. If you ever need the furniture moved or broken down, we can help out as well!

Our Mission

At DRS, our goal is to better serve, inspire and educate people in regards to custom commercial furniture and upholstery. Our blog gives us an opportunity to interact directly with you. Here, we share our expertise, our newest educational content, our most recent projects, and highlight some of favorite architects and designers.
